Sara Worldwide Business Pvt. Ltd.

View on full page →

Onsite

5 Openings

As a Digital Marketing Intern, you will assist the marketing team in executing campaigns across various digital channels. You’ll gain hands-on experience in SEO, social media, content creation, and analytics, learning how to grow a brand online.

Responsibilities of the Candidate:
  • Assist in the planning and execution of digital marketing campaigns
  • Create and schedule social media posts (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, etc.)
  • Perform keyword research and support SEO optimization
  • Write blog posts, email newsletters, and social media captions
  • Track and report on campaign performance using Google Analytics & other tools
  • Support in running and optimizing Google Ads and Meta Ads
  • Conduct market and competitor research
  • Help manage content calendars and campaign timelines
Requirements:
  • Basic understanding of digital marketing concepts (SEO, SEM, social media, content)
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills
  • Familiarity with tools like Google Analytics, Canva, or WordPress (a plus)
  • Eagerness to learn, take initiative, and work independently
  • Currently pursuing or recently completed a degree in Marketing, Business, Communication, or a related field
