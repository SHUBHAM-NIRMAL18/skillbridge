Job Description

As a Digital Marketing Intern, you’ll assist the marketing team in executing campaigns across various digital channels. You’ll gain hands-on experience in SEO, social media, content creation, and analytics, learning how to grow a brand online.

Responsibilities of the Candidate:
  • Assist in planning & execution of digital marketing campaigns
  • Create/schedule social media posts (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, etc.)
  • Perform keyword research & support SEO optimization
  • Write blog posts, email newsletters, and social captions
  • Track & report on campaign performance via Google Analytics & other tools
  • Support running/optimizing Google Ads and Meta Ads
  • Conduct market and competitor research
  • Manage content calendars and campaign timelines
Requirements
  • Basic understanding of digital marketing concepts (SEO, SEM, social media, content)
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills
  • Familiarity with tools like Google Analytics, Canva, or WordPress (a plus)
  • Eagerness to learn, take initiative, and work independently
  • Currently pursuing or recently completed a degree in Marketing, Business, Communication, or related field
